Tomato Egg Stir-Fry

Description

Tomato Egg Stir-Fry(西红柿炒鸡蛋) is one of the most traditional Chinese recipes that almost every place in China have it in every cuisine.

As it says, "You will never go wrong with egg and tomato combination." If you want to try any Chinese food, go for Tomato Egg Stir-Fry first!


Ingredients

Here is the ingredients for 2 servings.

  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 green onlin, chopped
  • 3 medium tomatoes, cut into 1-inch wedges
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon white sugar

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add eggs and green onions.
  3. Cook and stir until eggs are nearly set, about 10 seconds.
  4. Immediately scrape eggs into a bowl.
  5. Add tomatoes to the skillet.
  6. Saute, stirring occasionally, until juices are released and tomatoes are slightly wilted, about 5 minutes.
  7. Add salt and sugar; stir to mix well.
  8. Return eggs to skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, until eggs are set, 2 to 3 minutes.
