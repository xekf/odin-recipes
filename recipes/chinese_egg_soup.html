Chinese Egg Soup

Description

Whenever people in China talk about soup, it would be a big big topic that people can talk for a long time. Eating soup is more like a culture, especially in Southern China, for chinese in their daily meals. A soup could be very different in its ingredients, some are complicate that can have a dozen ingredients, while some could be as simple as only have 1 or 2 ingredients.

And this soup, Chinese Egg Soup(鸡蛋汤), is one of the most easiest soup you can make in less than 5 minutes.


Ingredients

  • 1 egg, beaten with a small amount of salt
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 3 teaspoon of oil
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. Bring oil and water in a large saucepan and boiled. Then put 1/2 teaspoon of salt in.
  2. Slowly add beaten egg to the boile water in a circle movemone above the saucepan.
  3. Wait for 5-10 secons, then slowly stir the egg for 10 secons.
  4. Put chopped green onions on the top of the soup, and serve hot.
